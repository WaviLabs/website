At Wavi Labs we provide a variety of resources ranging from SEO consulting to staff augmentation for your software needs. We pride ourselves in being able to provide great services at lower prices and faster times than our competitors.

Our Process

Consultation

During the consulting phase we get to know our client and their needs. We use this phase to make sure the client’s ideas are viable and that our services fit within their budget.

Prototype (Optional)

If the client doesn’t already have designs/mockups/prototypes that we discussed in the consultation phase we will begin the prototype phase. We will work closely with a client to develop a prototype product that acts as the basis for the development phase

Development

The development phase is the longest and most important phase. This is the phase where the project is implemented, going from idea to reality. This phase may be anywhere from 2 weeks to a year depending on the size of projects we are willing to accept at any given time. During this phase we are in constant contact with the client ensuring that the software meets the specification we decided upon in the consultation phase and listening to any requests for additional features that weren’t thought of initially.
